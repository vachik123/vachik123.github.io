about

I am currently an undergrad at Rutgers, where I triple major in Computer Science, Cognitive Science, and Linguistics, with a double minor in History and Philosophy.

In software engineering, I am an intern at Twitch (Amazon), where I work on the Twitch app on Android. Prior to that, I interned at Fidelity Investments, where I worked on the Fidelity Health app for iOS.

In research, I am a fellow at the Rutgers Center for Cognitive Science, where I research the intersection of language and cognition in news headlines, focusing on syntax-semantics interface of active vs passive voice - working with Shannon Bryant and Kristen Syrett, alongside four undergrads. Prior to that, I was a research assistant at the SHIRE (Social Human-AI Impressions & Representations) Lab, where I developed computational approaches to identifying intersectional biases in large language models.

I am also a developer at MeritMatch, a matchmaking platform that matches K12 schools with education vendors, eliminating cold calls and marketing emails.
